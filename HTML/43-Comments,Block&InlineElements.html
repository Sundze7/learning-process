Olufur Eliasson was born in Copenhagen, Demark in1967 to Iceland parents.

He is known for sculpture and large-scale installation art employing elemental materials such as light, water, and air temperature to enhance the viewer's experience

The dates for the ORIGIN OF ART exhibition are as follows:

  • Science: 21 Nov - 20 Feb 2010/11
  • Architecture: 6 March - 15 May 2011
  • History: 29 May - 21 Aug 2011
  • Religion: 28 Aug - 6 Nov 2011

Timed to a single revolution of the planet around the sun at a 23.4 degrees tilt that plays out the rhythm of the seasons, this Origins of Arts cycle is organized around four themes: science, architecture, history and religion
