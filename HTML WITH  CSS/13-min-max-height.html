Fender Mustang

The Fender Mustang was introduced in 1964 as the basis of a major redesign of Fender's student models then consisting of the Music-master and Duo-Sonic. It was originally popular in sixties surf music and attained cult status in the 1990's largely as a result of its use a number of alternative rock bands

Fender Sratocaster

The Fender Stratocaster or "Strat" is one of the most popular electric guitars of all time, and its design has been copied by many guitar maker. It was designed by Leo Fender, Goerge Fullerton and Fredie Tavares in 1954

Gibson Les Paul

The Gibson Les Paul is a solid body electric guitar that was first sold in 1952. The Les Paul was designed by Ted McCarity in collaboration with popular guitarist Les Paul, whom Gibson enlisted to endorse the new model. It was one of the most well-knowned electric guiter types in the world
